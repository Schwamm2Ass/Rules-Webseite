Schwamm2 Association (SM2A) – Official Rulebook

  • Point 1 – Game Mode & Field
    • Game mode: 7 vs 7 (6 field players + 1 goalkeeper)
    • Minimum players: At least 6 players (5 + 1) per team. No game start with fewer players. Minimum squad size: 11 players
    • Field: Half of a regular football field. One full-size goal remains. Second goal at midfield line. New center line marked. Outdoor (grass or artificial turf)
    • Substitutions: Maximum 4 stoppages, maximum 5 player changes per game, only during game stoppage
  • Point 2 – Federation Structure
    • Schwamm2 Association (SM2A) is the global federation
    • Each continent has a continental federation
    • National associations join a continental federation
    • Independent of FIFA and other organizations
  • Point 3 – Federation Meetings
    • 2 to 4 meetings per year
    • Quarterly or at least semi-annually
    • Online or on-site
    • Organization and further development
  • Point 4 – Cards & Penalties
    • Yellow Card – Warning
    • Yellow-Red Card – Send-off
    • Red Card – Send-off without replacement
    • Brown Card – 5-minute time penalty (once per game)
