Rules of the Continental Federation – Europe Schwamm2 Association (ESM2A)

  • 1. Official Regulations
    • The official regulations of the Schwamm2 Association (SM2A) apply to all member countries and national federations of ESA.
    • Exception Point 2: National federations joining ESA must be members of a European Union country.
    • Reason: Simplification of travel and administration, e.g., player participation only with ID card.
  • 2. Continental Competitions
    • Continental competitions may be held for all member federations according to the official rules of SM2A.
    • Master Cup: Introduced as a cup as soon as at least 2–3 national federations in Europe actively participate.
    • The execution, game mode, and organization will be determined once the competition is needed.
    • Goal: Promote European cohesion and sporting comparison among member federations.
  • 3. Money and Equipment
    • New clubs joining a national federation of ESA receive jerseys and training opportunities.
    • Details are implemented within the national federations.
    • For continental competitions, travel costs and, if applicable, accommodation may be covered upon request and approval.
    • Reason: Support the project’s development and encourage participation.
